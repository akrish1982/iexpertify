Compress PDF

Privacy: This tool runs fully in your browser. Your file is compressed locally and is never uploaded to any server.

How compression works: This tool re-encodes the PDF with stream compression, removes redundant internal objects, and applies lossless deflate compression to all content streams. Results vary by file — PDFs with uncompressed or loosely stored content see the biggest reductions.
